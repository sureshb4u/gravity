Arivanadh @ Manirthinam Architect/Director

He is a practising architect and director of gravity. he done his architecture degree at the renowned anna university. his thesis focused on creating visual and sensory narrative experiences by architecture for the five elements of nature contextually related to world’s most impactful rann of kutch after graduating he practised in various firms around india based on chennai, auroville, hyderabad.he had his hands on projects in terms of architecture and interiors for residential ,hospitality, commercial built typology. he got associated with brands such as ssn chennai, hns science centre bangalore, google hyderabad, iit hyderabad,dbs, ctrls s mumbai, kamaraj port limited and also with the common man’s needs in a form of shelter. he loves to the emphasis on honest use of materials and creating a memorable user experience by his spaces and transition of spaces inside and around the built environment.

Shanmuganathan Engineer/Director
